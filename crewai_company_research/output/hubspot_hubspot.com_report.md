## Products & Services

HubSpot offers a customer platform with a suite of software for marketing, sales, customer service, content management, data management, and commerce. The main products include:

- **Marketing Hub** - Comprehensive marketing automation and analytics
- **Sales Hub** - Sales CRM and pipeline management tools
- **Service Hub** - Customer service and support solutions
- **Content Hub** - Content management and optimization platform
- **Data Hub** - Data management and integration capabilities
- **Commerce Hub** - E-commerce and payment processing solutions

All products are built on an AI-powered Smart CRM that serves as the foundation of the platform.
